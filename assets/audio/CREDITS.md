# Charlie: Window Watch — sound credits

Every bundled recording is released under [Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/). Attribution is not required; it is included here with thanks to the creators.

## Charlie's barks

- `bark-front-door-1.mp3` and `bark-front-door-2.mp3` are trimmed from [Dog Whine and Bark.mp3](https://freesound.org/people/_natiKatz/sounds/530604/) by `_natiKatz`, featuring Annie the German Shepherd greeting visitors at her front door. Source: Freesound, CC0.
- `bark-indoor-1.mp3` and `bark-indoor-2.mp3` are trimmed from [Barking Dog Inside](https://bigsoundbank.com/barking-dog-inside-s0112.html) by Joseph SARDIN, a German Shepherd recorded barking indoors. Source: BigSoundBank, CC0.

## Owner, treat, and visitor sounds

- `shush-1.mp3` is trimmed from [sh sound effect (voice shh)](https://freesound.org/people/connerdrake98/sounds/701175/) by `connerdrake98`. Source: Freesound, CC0.
- `shush-2.mp3` is trimmed from [Shush!](https://freesound.org/people/OwlStorm/sounds/151224/) by OwlStorm / Ashe Kirk / Owlish Media. Source: Freesound, CC0.
- `chicken-crunch.mp3` is trimmed from [Dog Crunching Crackers](https://freesound.org/people/Bon_Vivant_Pictures/sounds/456366/) by Bon_Vivant_Pictures. Source: Freesound, CC0.
- `doorbell.mp3` is trimmed from [Mechanical doorbell #5](https://bigsoundbank.com/mechanical-doorbell-5-s2605.html) by Joseph SARDIN. Source: BigSoundBank, CC0.

## Game feedback

`guard-success.mp3`, `bark-hit.mp3`, `danger.mp3`, `power-up.mp3`, `room-switch.mp3`, and `ui-click.mp3` are converted from selected files in Kenney's [Interface Sounds 1.0](https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds) pack. Source: Kenney, CC0.

All source clips were trimmed where appropriate, faded to avoid clicks, level-balanced for the game, stripped of metadata, and converted to MP3. The original recordings are not redistributed in full.
